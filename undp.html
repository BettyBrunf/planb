In collaboration with UNDP Global Centre for Public Service Excellence we designed and published their book ‘Making Sustainable Development Goals Happen’ a compilation of 17 case studies of Public Service Excellence around the globe. With teams across Singapore and the United States we delivered graphic design, external communication and production services. As well as the design of the publication, we created a suite of content for their launch event, including social media posts and exhibition panels.
